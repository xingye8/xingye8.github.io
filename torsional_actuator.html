Soft Torsional Actuator

V-shape Pneumatic Torsional Actuator: A Building Block for Soft Grasper and Manipulator

Inspired by the fluid-driven origami style muscles, we introduce a soft pneumatic torsional actuator that generates large angular changes and twisting torques.

One of the unique features of our soft torsional actuator design is that they can be combined into multiple assemblies and therefore achieve sophisticated motions.

Applications

The torsional actuator acts just like a human joint.
We built a simplistic grasper to hold various objects commonly found in a laboratory.

Bimanual cooperation

Soft robotic hands and wrists unscrew a centrifuge tube and pour out the liquid inside of the tube.

Mixing Liquid With Pipette

By combining a linear module, a tilting module, and a grasping module, the soft manipulator can conduct a chemical experiment.
