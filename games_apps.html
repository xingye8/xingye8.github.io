An adventurous squirrel navigating challenging environments, enjoying his favorite acorns while evading deadly predators. A cross-platform multi-level game powered by libGDX. Written in Java and available on Windows and Android.

Read all of your news feed in one place! A news aggregator app that assembles news articles from various sources and presents them in a mobile-friendly view. Supports offline reading, bookmarks, reading history, search in page, sharing, and more. Available on Android.

Start learning a word and never forget! A language learning app that helps users study French vocabulary. The smart algorithm adjusts review frequency of each word based on familiarity so that the users are always on top of their memory decay curve. Offline learning mode is supported by the internal dictionary database. Available on Android.
